Mario Workout

  1. Mario: For Mario, jump and swing one arm behind You. and the other arm up above Your head.
  2. Banzai bill: For Banzai bill, lift Your legs and Your chest and head of the ground.
  3. Fliprus: For Fliprus, jump up and then move Your arms like You are throwing a snow ball.
  4. Goomba: For Goomba, jump and then run like You are chasing after someone. repeat this pattern.
  5. Bowser: For Bowser, walk slowly and then jump as high as You can. repeat this pattern.
  6. Burt the bashful: For Burt the bashful, crouch and then jump as high as You can. repeat this pattern
  7. Hammer bro: For Hammer bro, move Your hand up and down and jump to different places.
  8. Walleye: For Walleye, move side to side and pause for 1 second at each side.
