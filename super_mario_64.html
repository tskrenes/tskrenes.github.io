Super mario 64

Super mario 64 was released in 1996 for the nintendo 64(N64). It is a 3d game in witch
mario goes through levels, hidden in paintings, to rescue princess peach.

Super mario 64

Developer: Nintendo EAD
Publisher: Nintendo
System: N64
Release Date: june 23, 1996

Story

Mario gets a letter witch says: Dear Mario, please come to the castle. I've
baked a cake for you. Yours truly, Princess Peach.
How exiting! Mario runs of to the castle, but when he enters is a gruff
voice says: No one's home! Now scram! Bwa, ha, ha. In this
game mario looks for power stars and uses them to enter locked doors.

Gameplay

Unlike other mario games this one is 3d. Mario can defeat enemies not just
by jumping on them, but also by kicking and punching them. You get power
stars by finding them in various levels and by beating bosses.

controls

  • use the control stick to Move Mario/cursor, climb poles, angle camera in second-person mode and fly (when wearing the Wing Cap)
  • press A to jump, swim and confirm
  • press B to punch, kick, dive, grab, throw or cancel
  • press Z to crouch
  • press left or right to move camera
  • press up to Zoom in or enter second-person mode (which allows the player to look around)
  • press down to zoom out
  • press R to toggle camera mode
  • press start to Pause the game, show Power Star list (when in the castle) or pause menu (when in courses)
  • While running, use the control stick to quickly turn around + A to do a sideways somersault
  • upon hitting a wall, press A to do a wall jump
  • jump and then upon landing jump to do a double jump
  • jump and then upon landing jump and then jump again to do a triple jump (while running) or lift of(wearing wing cap)
  • press A and B to do a jump kick(from a standstill) or midair dive(while running)
  • press B three times in a row to do a punch punch kick
  • while in midair, press Z to do a ground pound
  • press Z and use the control stick to crawl
  • press Z and a to do a backwards somersault(from a standstill) or long jump(while running)
  • press Z and B to do a Sweep Kick (from a standstill) or a Slide Kick (while running)
