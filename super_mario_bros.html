Super Mario Bros.

Super mario bros. is a video game released for the NES(Nintendo Entertainment System) and Family Computer(Famicom) in 1985. You can also play it in the
game Super mario all stars for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System(SNES).
Super mario bros. is a side-scrolling platformer game created by Nintendo in 1985 originally for the NES.

Developer: Nintendo EAD
Publisher: Nintendo
System: NES/Famicom
Release Date: september 13, 1985

story

One day the peaceful mushroom kingdom was invaded by bowser and his minions.
All the citizens of the kingdom got turned into bricks and blocks. The mushroom kingdom fell into ruins.
The only one who can undo the the evil spell was princess toadstool but bowser has caught her.
it is up to mario (or luigi if you are player 2) to save the princess.

Gameplay

Super mario bros. has 8 worlds with 4 levels. Mario or luigi have to get to the
end of the level by jumping over various gaps and dodging enemies.

controls

  • press left or right on the d pad to move or change positions on a beanstalk
  • press down on the d pad to duck or enter a pipe
  • press down or up on the d pad to climb a beanstalk
  • press A to jump or swim upwards
  • press B to dash or throw a fireball or restart the game at the end or select a world
  • press start to pause or confirm selected option on the title screen
  • press select to select number of players on the title screen
  • press start while holding down A to continue in the same world after the Game Over screen
