Super Mario Bros.

Super mario bros. is a video game released for the NES(Nintendo Entertainment System) and Family Computer(Famicom) in 1985. You can also play it in the
game Super mario all stars for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System(SNES).
Super mario bros. is a side-scrolling platformer game created by Nintendo in 1985 originally for the NES.

story

One day the peaceful mushroom kingdom was invaded by bowser and his minions.
All the citizens of the kingdom got turned into bricks and blocks. The mushroom kingdom fell into ruins.
The only one who can undo the the evil spell was princess toadstool but bowser has caught her.
it is up to mario (or luigi if you are player 2) to save the princess.

Gameplay

Super mario bros. has 8 worlds with 4 levels. Mario or luigi have to get to the
end of the level by jumping over various gaps and dodging enemies.

controls

  • press left or right on the d pad to move or change positions on a beanstalk
  • press down on the d pad to duck or enter a pipe
  • press down or up on the d pad to climb a beanstalk
  • press A to jump or swim upwards
  • press B to dash or throw a fireball or restart the game at the end or select a world
  • press start to pause or confirm selected option on the title screen
  • press select to select number of players on the title screen
  • press start while holding down A to continue in the same world after the Game Over screen

hard mode

After you beat the game you can choose to select a world (by pressing B
on the title screen) to play in "a new quest" a mode where all the
goombas are replaced by buzzy beetles and all the lifts are about 60%
smaller then they used to be.

glitches

minus world

The Minus World is an endless underwater level, identical to World 7-2, which is accessed through a glitch. There is no way to complete the level, as entering the pipe
at the end will simply return Mario or Luigi to the one at the beginning. To perform the glitch, Mario must go to World 1-2 and stand on top of the pipe that leads to the flag,
without going in the pipe. Then he must break the second and third block from the pipe, but leave the one on the far right. Then he must stand on the left edge of
the pipe (facing left) and duck. He then has to jump while in a ducking position and move right in mid-air (while still facing left). If done correctly, Mario will go through the
block on the far right and the wall to the Warp Zone. Mario must then go through the pipe that would normally lead to World 2-1 or World 4-1, and Mario will enter the Minus World.
If World 2-2 or 7-2 is edited in a ROM editor so that it is possible to beat it during the underwater segment, there is a World -2, an underwater version of 3-4. If World 3-4 is
edited in a ROM editor, then there is a World -3. It is also a version of 3-4, but with overworld graphics; beating World -3 takes the player to World -4, a level which is just a blank screen.

over the flagpole

Over the flagpole is a glitch first introduced in super mario bros. If you jump over the flagpole their is just an endless path.
to do this in level 3-3 jump onto the lift at the end and wait until the other side rises. Don't let it get to high or you cant jump onto it.
After that jump onto the other side and without it moving jump over the finish.

warps

1-2

The warp in level 1-2 is hidden at the end of the level. Jump onto the last red platform at the end
of the level wait until it reaches the top. Now jump onto the bricks above the pipe that takes you
to the finish. Keep running until you reach a set of pipes. you can now select world 2, 3, or 4.

4-2 - world 6, 7, 8

The last warp is hidden in level 4-2. After you jump of the first red platform, there will be 3 blocks
above you. The one closest to the red lift has a vine in it. There are also 5 invisible blocks hit them
in the right order to reach to block containing the vine. Hit the block with the vine and climb it.
Once you jump across the mushroom platforms at the top, there will be 3 pipes. Select world 6, 7, or 8.

4-2 - world 5

To warp to world 5, stand on the first red platform and stay on the top of the level.
Once at the end of the level you will find a room with a pipe. enter the pipe to go to world 5.
