Super Mario Odyssey Speedrun Guide

This is a guide on how to speedrun mario odyssey.

Tips: before speedruning, make sure you set the language to japanese(3rd from the bottom). The reason is because japanese has the least words.
In the moon kingdom, you can skip the moon caves(which saves a LOT of time!). To do it, at the quiet wall checkpoint head to the right where the sphinx can be found.
Get on its head and ground pound jump off of it. Next, perform a cap throw and hold and run into cappy(remember, do this in the air) to bounce off of him,
then wall jump off of an L shape on the wall, then bounce off of the other wall and dive onto the ledge in front of you.
Another tip is to play in 2 player mode. If the moon cave skip is too hard, in 2 player mode, the moon caves only take about 10 minutes(which is 10 minutes added to your speedrun time).
In the lake kingdom, you will find some broken stairs. Triple jump and then cap dive to get on top of them(skipping the spiky waterway).
Make sure you skip any cutscenes (by pressing +). Some of them are quite long.
When doing the darker side speedrun, you can skip the whole underground part with a simple glitch.
To do it, you must capture a frog, jump, un-capture the frog, bounce on another frog's head and capture it at the same time. You can then swim through the air. The glitch ends when you un-capture the frog, or jump.

Speedrun Rules

Speedrun starts whey you start a new file and ends when you zoom away on the spark pylon at the end. Beat the game as fast as you can and add any challenges you want to (although these count a different speedrun). For challenges see this. For types of speedruns see this.

Moves


Player One Controls


Run

The move you need most is running. As you can guess, you run by pushing the control stick in any direction.

Walk

You can walk in the same way you run, except you push the control stick more lightly.

Jump

You can't beat the game without jumping! To jump just press A or B.

Cap Throw

You can can do a cap throw by pressing X or Y. If this is done in midair, it will slow Mario's fall a little bit. You can also cap throw by doing with the joycons what you would when actually throwing a cap.

Capture

To capture, you must perform a cap throw near an enemy without a hat. If one does have a hat, you can knock it off the same way you capture(remember, captures only work on some enemies).

Spin

To spin, rotate the control stick around. Mario will start spin around. Spins can slow mario's falling time a little bit.

Downwards Throw

If you downwards throw, cappy will zoom downward. To downward throw ether whip the joycons downward, or ground pound and then throw cappy.

Upwards Throw

Upward throws are the opposite of downward throws. This time, to perform an upwards throw you can only whip the joycons up.

Ground Pound

To ground pound, press ZL in midair. Ground pounds can find things hidden in the ground as well as quicken your falling speed. Ground pounds can also pound things into the ground.

Ground Pound Jump

Ground pound jumps can get higher than jumps. To ground pound jump, ground pound, then jump as soon as you hit the ground.

Spin Jump

Spin jumps are what they sound like. To spin jump, spin, then jump.

Spin Throw

If your surrounded by enemies and wish you could just kill them all at once, spin throws are for you. To spin throw, ether spin and do a cap throw or whip the joycons around you.

Homing Cap Throw

If your aim is a little off, a homing cap throw will do the trick. To do a homing cap throw, perform a cap throw and shake the joycons.

Crouch

With crouching, you can access areas you otherwise couldn't. To crouch, press ZL.

Roll

Rolling is a little bit faster then running. To roll, you must crouch and then continue pressing Y(X also works).

Ledge Climb

By ledge climbing, you can hold onto a ledge. To ledge climb, approach a ledge at a slow speed. Mario will grab onto the ledge.

Pole Climb

With pole climbing, you can climb poles. To pole climb, jump onto a pole. Press B(or A) at any time when climbing the pole to jump off. At the top of the pole, press A or B to flip off.

Cap Throw And Hold

These are just a different kind of cap throw. To perform one, press and hold X (or Y).

Wall Jump

To wall jump, jump at a wall and press B or A as soon as Mario touches the wall. If there are 2 walls next to each other(and they are close enough), repeat this to get very high.

Cap Jump

Cap jumping can get you to higher places than jumps can. To cap jump press and hold Y(or X), then run into the cap. It will give you a large boost.

Crouch Walk

To crouch walk, hold ZL and then move the control stick in any direction. It is slower than walking, but you can walk into small places.

Dive

While in midair, press ZL and Y or X. It gives you a little boost forward in the air but keep in mind that you can not do anything once you dive.

Cap Dive

Cap dives can get much higher than jumps. To cap dive, jump, cap throw, and then dive onto the cap.

Long Jump

With long jumps, you can not get as high as jumps but you can get farther than jumps. To long jump, press ZL and B(or A) while running.

Double Jump

To double jump, jump and then as soon as you hit the ground jump again.

Triple Jump

To triple jump, double jump and the jump again as soon as you hit the ground. You must be moving at least mario's walking speed to triple jump. Triple jumps are the highest kind of jump.


Player Two Controls


Move

When playing as cappy, moving is a little different. First press X or Y, then you can move the control stick in any direction you want. Press X(or Y) to return to mario. The following moves have to be done while flying.

Jump

The controls for jumping as cappy are the same as jumping as mario: just press A. If you're on mario's head and press A or B, you can cap bounce mario (which does not count as jumping but is the same hight as a jump).

Ground Pound

Ground pounding can be done by jumping and then pressing ZL. They do the same things as mario's ground pounds.



Power Moons


Cascade Kingdom


Our First Power Moon

You don't need any power moons in the cap kingdom, so the next kingdom is the cascade kingdom. To find this moon, you must capture the chain chomp and smash the glowing rocks behind it.

Behind The Waterfall

After breaking the wall containing the 8-bit section, head up to the top and hit the hidden block next to the waterfall. Then jump onto it and grab the moon behind the falls.

Multi Moon Atop The Falls

The missions objective is to defeat madame broode on top of the waterfall. To beat her, you must knock off the cap on her "adorable chain chompikins" then capture it. She will then try to smack you. If she does, you will un-capture the chain chomp. After a little while she will get tired of trying to smack you, then pull back on the control stick and let go. After beating her, you get a multi moon which is 3 power moons.


Sand Kingdom


Employees Only

This power moon is found inside the crazy cap shop. To get it, you must go to the back of the building where you will find a small hole in which you must crouch walk into. You will find yourself in the back of the crazy cap store where a power moon can be found.

A Rumble From The Sandy Floor

Outside the crazy cap shop, you can find an umbrella which you must bounce on to get onto the roof. There will be a pipe which will take you to an area where you have to ground pound on the place where the joycons rumble most(which is in the top left corner). The prize is a power moon.

Overlooking The Desert Town

This power moon is found on top of the large building in the left half of the town.

Inside The Block Is A Hard Place

This power moon is found a little south of the tostarena ruins sand pillar. It is in a glowing block which you must break with a bullet bill.

On The Leaning Pillar

You must capture a bullet bill and use it to collect the power moon on one of the leaning pillars.

Alcove In The Ruins

Like the name says, it is found in an alcove in the ruins west of the tostarena ruins sand pillar checkpoint.

From A Crate In The Ruins

Next to the bullet bill cannon south of the tostarena ruins round tower, you can find a glowing crate which you must ground pound on to receive a power moon which will land on top of a ledge south of the crate.

Atop The Highest Tower

To get this power moon, you must traverse the ruins a little bit past the town. The moon is found after the 8-bit section.

Moon Shards In The Sand

To get this moon, you have to collect all of the moon shards in the moe-eye habitat which are found on an invisible platform above the poison, on top of one of the towers in the poison, between 2 cacti, on a hill, and next to a moe-eye.

Lost In The Luggage

Before entering the inverted pyramid, there will be a glowing spot surrounded by boxes and tyres. If you ground pound on it, a power moon will appear.

Showdown On The Inverted Pyramid

The object of this mission is to fight hariet on top of the inverted pyramid by knocking back the bombs she throws(by throwing cappy at it) and then jumping on her head. By doing this, you get a multi moon.

The Hole In The Desert

You must defeat knucklotec in a hole underneath the inverted pyramid after it rises up. To defeat him, you must lure one of his hands onto a patch of ice and capture it when he is stunned, and then punch him in the face. After doing this, you get a multi moon.

Lake Kingdom


On The Lake Shore

When you first land in the lake kingdom, you will find a glowing spot next to the odyssey. You will receive a power moon if you ground pound on it.

Cheep Cheep Crossing

This power moon can be found under the underwater entrance checkpoint.

Dorrie-Back Rider

This power moon can be found on dorrie's back.

Broodals Over The Lake

This missions objective is to defeat rango on top of a large stone structure on top of the lake. To beat him, throw you cap at his and bounce off of the flower on his hat(now exposed), then jump on his head.

Whats In The Box?

On top of the water plaza, there will be a platform with a glowing crate on it. If you pound on it, you will receive a power moon.

End Of A Hidden Passage

I know this moon sounds hard, but it is actually pretty easy. At the start, you can find a platform with a hat flower on it. Bounce off of it and onto another platform containing a zipper which you can capture. After un-zipping it, a hidden passage will be revealed with a power moon at the end.

Challenges


Blindfold Challenge

You must have played the game once to speedrun it blindfolded! To do this challenge, you must speedrun the game first, and then play it blindfolded. Combine the two and you get this challenge.


No Jumping Challenge

You may think that it is impossible to not jump, but cap jumping does not count as jumping(also, jumping in captures does not count as jumping ether) so this challenge isn't to hard is it? Well... your jumps are a little delayed but other than that nothings different.


No Cap Throw Challenge

Out of all of the challenges, this one is one of the hardest. Even harder than the blindfold challenge. Wait, if you can't cap throw you can't capture right? Well no. After cap bouncing in 2 player mode or cap jumping in 1 player mode, cappy stays there for a little bit. So if you get the distance just right, you can still capture.


No Coins Challenge

This challenge is one of the easiest. Its a little bit hard because coins sometimes come out of places you wouldn't even think of and you're bound to collect at least one coin on your way to the boss.


No Jumping, No Cap Throw, No Coins Challenge

This challenge is almost impossible. You will need to create a copy of your file(when you haven't jumped, thrown cappy, or collected any coins) because you probably do one of these things on the way to the boss.



Types Of Speedruns


Any%

The most common speedrun. In this speedrun, it doesn't mater how many bosses you beat, how many moons you collect or anything like that. All you have to do is complete the main game.

All Moons

In this speedrun, you have to collect all the moons in the game as fast as you can.

100%

This speedrun is the hardest one to do. The fastest speedrun is 8 hours, 48 minutes, and 45 seconds. You must collect all of the moons and regional coins.

Challenge Speedrun

Add any challenge to your speedrun (challenges shown above) and beat the game as fast as you can.

Dark Side

Get to and beat the dark side. You must collect 250 power moons before you can get to the dark side.

Darker Side

The darker side requires 500 power moons to reach it. Once there, you must collect the multi moon at the end.

World Peace

You must complete all of the story missions and then beat bowser.

Challenge Speedrun 2

This is sort of a weird speedrun. In this, you must complete a certain amount of challenges (like collecting 3 painting moons, 5 moons buried in the ground, etc).

Talkatoo%

In this speedrun, you can only get moons that Talkatoo says. Remember, he only gives the names of 3 moons at a time.



Mods

Mods modify the game and usually create extra challenges. This section will show you most of the mods. For a full list of mods, check out this page.

Custom Levels Mod

The custom levels mod has custom levels people created (like Bianco Hills, Bob-omb Battlefield etc).

The Floor Is Lava Mod

This mod is very hard. The whole floor is lava. Things sticking out of the lava do not have lava on top of them. Also, there are some platforms that are added to make the game beatable.

Superstar Mode Mod

If you find the game too easy and want a little challenge, try superstar mode. This makes the game a "little bit" harder. For example, the first madame broode boss is the same as the regular moon kingdom. And the sand kingdom's floor is lava!

Underwater Mod

In this mod, the whole game is underwater. No matter how high you swim, you can never reach the top. But power moons refill your health and air.

Gravity Mod

This (for me) is the most fun mod. In this mod, the gravity switches every 30 seconds. For whatever reason, the game's north, west, south, and east are different than on the compass. Gravity does not switch in captures.

Bad Cappy Mod

So in this mod, you can not walk or run with cappy on your head (you can in captures). But whats the "bad" part? Well, after you throw cappy, you can move but when cappy reaches mario, mario dies! Cappy now can get stuck behind things and moves slower. Also, cappy's distance behind mario is shown (in metres).
