Super Mario World

Super Mario World is a 2d platformer game released for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System(SNES)
in 1990. you can also play it in Super Mario All Stars + Super Mario World also for the SNES.

Super mario world

Developer: Nintendo EAD
Publisher: Nintendo
System: SNES
Release Date: november 21, 1990

Story

Mario and his friends decide to take a vacation on Dinosaur island. Meanwhile on Dinosaur island ,
bowser and his koopa crew are planning an attack! They trap yoshi and his friends in eggs! Shortly
after, Mario and Luigi find that princess peach is missing. As they look mario finds an egg. Yoshi hatches
out of it. Yoshi tells mario that bowser trapped his friends in eggs. Yoshi hands mario a cape he says it
has magic powers that will help mario on his journey. Its time for a new adventure!

Gameplay

The 9 worlds have usually 6 levels. There are 92 levels in total.
The object is to get to the giant gate at the end of each level.
Some of the levels have secrets. The levels with secrets are red on the world map.

controls

  • press left or right on the d pad to move
  • press down on the d pad to crouch
  • press up on the d pad to look up
  • press A to do a spin jump
  • press B to jump
  • press X or Y to dash or use a special ability
  • press L to scroll camera left (does not work in auto-scrolling levels,
    Yoshi's House, Top Secret Area, or Boss Rooms)
  • press R to scroll camera left (does not work in auto-scrolling levels,
    Yoshi's House, Top Secret Area, or Boss Rooms)
  • press start to pause
  • press select to use item or return to world map from a completed level (when paused)
